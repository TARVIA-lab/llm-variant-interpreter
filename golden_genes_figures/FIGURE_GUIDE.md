## 🏆 Figure 3: Top Golden Genes (392 KB)
**File:** `Fig3_Top_Golden_Genes.png`

### What It Shows:
The 30 strongest golden genes ranked by two different metrics.

**Panel A: By Spheroid Upregulation**
- Top 30 genes sorted by how much they're upregulated in spheroids
- Green bars = logFC < -3 (very strong)
- Orange bars = logFC -2 to -3 (strong)
- Blue bars = logFC -1 to -2 (moderate)
- Red dashed lines mark critical thresholds

**Panel B: By RelB Dependency**
- Top 30 genes sorted by how much they depend on RelB
- Red bars = logFC < -3 in RelB-KD (very dependent)
- Orange bars = logFC -2 to -3

- Shows which genes fall hardest when RelB is knocked out

### Key Interpretation:
- **Genes at top of both panels** = highest priority targets
- **Example:** APOA4, RP11-433M22.2 are highly dependent on both spheroid context and RelB
- **Compare Panel A vs B** = see genes that are spheroid-specific vs RelB-specific
