KRAS p.Gly12Asp (G12D) is a well-established oncogenic driver mutation constitutively activating the RAS/MAPK pathway. Unlike G12C, no FDA-approved direct inhibitor exists for G12D; however, downstream MEK inhibitors and combination strategies are in clinical trials.
